Safety Measures

Before And After Titans Arrivals

Develop a comprehensive plan that includes escape routes, safe zones, and emergency contacts.
Identify and fortify safe havens where people can seek refuge from the titans
Implement warning & alarms to alert the community about the impending arrival of titans
Stock up on essential supplies such as non-perishable food, water and first aid kits
Establish a reliable communication system with your group to coordinate and share critical information
If the situation becomes untenable, follow the evacuation plan to move to a safer location
After the threat has passed, focus on rebuilding and recovery efforts. Assess damage and prioritize reconstruction
